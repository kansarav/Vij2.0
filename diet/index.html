Greek Egg White Masala Scramble
Anti-inflammatory · high protein · psoriasis-fighter
510 kcal48g protein35g carbs18g fat
▾
Ingredients
  • Whole eggs2 large (100g)
  • Egg whites4 (120g)
  • Low-fat paneer, crumbled80g
  • Spinach, fresh80g
  • Tomato, diced1 medium (100g)
  • Onion, fine dice½ small (40g)
  • Green chilli, sliced1
  • Ginger, grated1 tsp (5g)
  • Cumin seeds½ tsp
  • Turmeric¼ tsp
  • Garam masala¼ tsp
  • Ghee1 tsp (5g)
  • Whole wheat roti1 (40g)
Method
  1. 1Heat ghee on medium. Cumin crackles 30 sec. Add onion + ginger + chilli — 3 min.
  2. 2Add tomato + turmeric — cook 3 min until broken down. Add spinach, wilt 2 min.
  3. 3Add crumbled paneer, stir to coat.
  4. 4Whisk eggs + whites, pour over. Fold gently — remove from heat while slightly wet.
  5. 5Finish with garam masala + salt. Serve with 1 roti.
VIJ TIP: Black pepper with turmeric boosts curcumin absorption by 2000%. Ghee's fat carries it further. Psoriasis double-win.

Shakshuka with Paneer
Middle Eastern · lycopene-rich · one-pan wonder
500 kcal40g protein36g carbs22g fat
▾
Ingredients
  • Whole eggs3
  • Paneer, cubed 2cm100g
  • Chopped tomatoes (tin or fresh)400g
  • Red bell pepper, diced1 large (120g)
  • Onion, diced1 medium (80g)
  • Garlic4 cloves, sliced
  • Cumin seeds1 tsp
  • Smoked paprika1 tsp
  • Harissa paste / red chilli1 tsp
  • Turmeric¼ tsp
  • EVOO1 tbsp
  • Fresh coriander + feta crumbleto finish
  • Whole wheat pita / roti1 (50g)
Method
  1. 1Heat EVOO. Cumin crackles. Add onion — 5 min. Add garlic + pepper — 3 min.
  2. 2Add harissa, paprika, turmeric — 1 min. Add tomatoes. Simmer 12 min until thick sauce.

Egg Fried Brown Rice
Uses leftover rice · wok-fast · protein-loaded
580 kcal36g protein64g carbs18g fat
▾
Ingredients
  • Cooked cold brown rice (day-old)200g
  • Whole eggs3
  • Egg whites2
  • Edamame / frozen peas60g
  • Carrots, fine dice60g
  • Spring onions4
  • Garlic + ginger2 cloves + 1 tsp
  • Tamari / low-sodium soy2 tbsp
  • Sesame oil1 tsp (finish)
  • Coconut oil (high-heat cooking)1 tbsp
  • Toasted sesame seeds1 tbsp
  • Chilli oil / srirachato taste
Method
  1. 1Wok (or wide pan) on maximum heat — smoking hot. Add coconut oil.
  2. 2Whisk eggs + whites. Pour in wok — scramble 45 sec into large pieces. Remove. Set aside.
  3. 3More heat — add garlic + ginger 30 sec. Add carrot 2 min. Add cold rice — spread flat, don't stir 2 min (forms crust). Then toss.
  4. 4Add edamame + tamari. Toss 2 min. Return eggs + white parts of spring onion.
  5. 5Off heat: sesame oil + green spring onion tops + sesame seeds + chilli.
THE KEY: Day-old cold rice is non-negotiable. Fresh rice has too much moisture and turns to mush. Always cook extra brown rice and refrigerate for next-day fried rice. The crust that forms is called "wok hei" — the hallmark of great fried rice.
